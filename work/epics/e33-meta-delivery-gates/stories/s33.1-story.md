# S33.1 — Knowledge: Meta Delivery System

## User Story

As Kokoro (ads analysis tool), I need a knowledge file documenting Meta's ad
delivery system phases and thresholds, so that I can evaluate whether an ad has
sufficient data before recommending on/off decisions.

## Acceptance Criteria

- **Given** a knowledge file, **when** I read delivery phases, **then** I find
  exploration, learning, and optimization with clear definitions
- **Given** threshold data, **when** I check learning phase exit criteria,
  **then** I find 50 conv/7d (standard) and 10 conv/3d (Purchase)
- **Given** practical thresholds, **when** I check kill/pause rules, **then** I
  find hard kill (3x CPA), soft pause (72h + 30% above CPA), minimum 5 days
- **Given** the 4 Meta AI systems, **when** I check their role in delivery,
  **then** I find how Andromeda/GEM/Lattice/Sequence affect exploration and
  learning
