## Observations

- Source material has significant overlap between notas-eduardo and workbook versions.
  Curation (not copying) was the right call — structured extraction avoids duplication.
- The master file serves as a router guide — it doesn't duplicate content from modules
  but provides the decision tree and interaction map.
- Extension mirrors ensure knowledge is available in both host and extension contexts.
- conocimientoraiz/ is gitignored — workbook 09 was copied but won't be tracked.

## Patterns

- **Content curation > content copying** — Structured extraction with frameworks and
  anti-patterns is more useful for AI consumption than raw transcripts.
