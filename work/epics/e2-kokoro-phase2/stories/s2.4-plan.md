### T2: GREEN — Create kokoro-interviews.md skill file

**Goal:** Create the skill file with Eduardo's voice, Problem and Solution Interview
sections, coaching methodology, bias awareness, quality metrics, anti-patterns, and
summary template. All T1 tests pass.

**Steps:**

1. Create `extension/.claude/commands/kokoro-interviews.md` with:
   - Opening quote in Eduardo's voice: "No midas lo que los consumidores dicen que haran, mide lo que hicieron."
   - `## Contexto` — purpose, behaviors over intentions, validation as evidence
   - `## Antes de Empezar — Estrategia del Proyector` — ask permission, set context, 70/30 rule
   - `## Entrevista de Problema` — full section with:
     - `### Preparacion e Invitacion` — selecting invitados, no-selling framing, 20-min ask
     - `### Estructura de la Sesion` — 4 steps (context, narrative, active listening, close)
     - `### Preguntas Poderosas` — questions for problem discovery + Customer Forces discovery
     - `### Lo que NUNCA hacer` — anti-patterns (selling, leading questions, future intentions)
   - `## Analisis Post-Entrevistas` — patterns, persona update, forces canvas update
     - `### Sesgos a Vigilar` — confirmation, social desirability, recency, availability
   - `## Entrevista de Solucion` — when to do it (after 10 problem interviews), structure, observation > words
   - `## Metodologia de Coaching en Entrevistas` — coach not consultant, question-first
   - `## Metricas de Calidad` — what a good interview round produces
   - `## Resumen` — markdown table template for interview preparation and findings
   - Reference to `kokoro-phase2-interviews.md` knowledge file
   - Eduardo's vocabulary throughout (invitado, creacion, inversion — never cliente, producto, precio)
2. Each major section includes:
   - Explanation of purpose
   - At least 2 guiding questions (powerful questions, past-behavior focus)
   - Eduardo's coaching perspective
   - Interactive prompt: wait for entrepreneur's input before proceeding
